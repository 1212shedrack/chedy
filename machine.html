machine Learning:

Machine learning is a subset of artificial intelligence that enables computers to learn from and make decisions based on data without explicit programming. It involves the development of algorithms that can identify patterns and make predictions or classifications from new data. Common machine learning algorithms include linear regression for predicting continuous values, decision trees for hierarchical decision-making, support vector machines (SVM) for classification tasks by finding the optimal boundary between classes, k-nearest neighbors (KNN) for classifying data points based on their nearest neighbors, and neural networks for complex pattern recognition in tasks such as image and speech recognition. These algorithms are applied across various domains to enhance decision-making, automate processes, and uncover insights from large datasets.
